About Us

Partnering with contractors to make their job a success through quality equipment, industry expertise, and responsive service.

Water Truck Supply was created to provide a professional water truck rental and sale experience to contractors across Texas and the United States. Our true north is partnering with contractors to make their job a success through quality equipment, industry expertise, and responsive service.

Because we are not a dealership, we are able to offer construction companies the most productive solution for their job regardless of brand. We understand that water trucks can be one of the most important pieces of equipment on the jobsite, and when your water truck is down the whole job can be down.
